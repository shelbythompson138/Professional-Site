Teaching Philosophy🔍

As a Language Arts teacher and future Library Media Specialist, I believe in empowering students to become confident readers, critical thinkers, and lifelong learners. I strive to create an inclusive, student-centered environment where literacy is the foundation for inquiry, communication, and personal growth. By integrating diverse texts, research skills, and digital tools, I aim to make learning meaningful and relevant. I view my role as a facilitator who supports student voice, curiosity, and collaboration, helping each learner thrive in both academic and real-world settings.
