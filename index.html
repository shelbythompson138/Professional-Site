Meet the Author!

Hello! My name is Shelby Thompson and I am a passionate educator in London, Ky. This professional website will serve as a place for you to follow along with my journey and professional career.

I currently teach 6th grade Language Arts at Corbin Middle School. Teaching students to become readers, writers, and leaders gives me great purpose in life. After school, I work as the Academic Team Coach. Giving an avenue for gifted students to showcase thier skills and shine bright is a great privilege.

Why this Site?

On this website you will find a section about me, my teaching blog, and a section dedictaed to my teacher must-haves and favorite teaching strategies. Each of these will serve as a window into my teaching journey and journey towards becoming a School Librarian.

Misson Statement

As a passionate educator, I believe every student deserves access to inclusive, engaging learning experiences that foster curiosity, literacy, and confidence. My mission is to empower students through reading, research, and creativity—both in the classroom and beyond.
